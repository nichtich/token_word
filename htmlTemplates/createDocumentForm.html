create document:

separate paragraphs with a blank line. The first paragraph will be used as the title of your document (in other words, separate the title from the rest of your document with a blank line).

you can embed quotes from your quote clipboard in your document with <q N>-style quote tags, where N is the quote number. For example, inserting the tag <q 3> would embed quote 3 in your document.

other HTML-style tags are not supported and will be stripped out in a rather crude manner---do not use them.

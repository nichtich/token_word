extract quote:
insert <q> </q> tags around the text you want to quote. For example, inserting tags <q>like this</q> would select the words like this as your quote.

you can only insert one pair of tags (in other words, around one contiguous region). Extracting multiple quotes simultaneously is not currently supported.

except for inserting quote tags, you should leave the document text unaltered. Inserting other characters into the document will have undesireable effects on the document region selected as your quote.

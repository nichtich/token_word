extract quote:
insert <q> </q> tags around the text you want to quote. For example, inserting tags <q>like this</q> would select the words like this as your quote.

to extract multiple quotes simultaneously, insert more than one pair of tags. For example, inserting tags <q>like this</q> and <q>also like this</q> would select the words like this as one quote and the words also like this as another quote.

except for inserting quote tags, you should leave the document text unaltered. Inserting other characters into the document will have undesirable effects on the document regions selected as your quotes.
